   - Note: No separate notification permission request needed here because the `RequestNotificationPermissionAndStartPolling` composable in MainActivity will fire once isConfigured flips to true (which happens when setup saves tokens). However, there is a race: the composable recomposition from DataStore may not be instant. So we DO start the service here explicitly to ensure immediate polling. On Android 13+ without the permission granted yet, `startForegroundService` will work but the notification may be suppressed -- the permission composable in MainActivity will handle that shortly after.
   - Actually, a cleaner approach: Do NOT request permission in RelayNavGraph. Just call `startPollingService(context)` directly. The `RequestNotificationPermissionAndStartPolling` in MainActivity handles the permission side. On pre-13, this just works. On 13+, the service starts (foreground service works without notification permission -- the notification just won't show). The MainActivity composable will prompt for permission immediately after navigation.
